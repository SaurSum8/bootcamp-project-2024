CalHacks 11.0

Just returned from my first hackathon, CalHacks 11.0. It was an absolute blast!
Even though we weren't able to fully finish our planned project, I had lots of fun working with my team.
We tried to make a voluntary computational resource sharing platform, AirPool.
Anyone could share their ideas, and the code needed to be computed. This could be large data analysis project, huge neural networks, etc.
Donors would then vote and choose to donate their computer power to the project of their choice.
This project would greatly help startups or small developer teams that have little to no money, but are working on ambitious projects!
